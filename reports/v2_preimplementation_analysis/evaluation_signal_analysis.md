## Does Evaluation Evidence Predict Quality?

Yes, directionally and qualitatively. The committee repeatedly used evaluation evidence as a trust signal because the JD specifically requires offline benchmarks, online A/B testing, recruiter-feedback loops, NDCG, MRR, and recall@K.

Evaluation evidence is strongest when it appears in career descriptions with ownership verbs and production/search context. Generic model evaluation is weaker. V2 should make evaluation/relevance science a separate feature family rather than treating it as incidental text inside technical relevance.

## V2 Implication

Promote candidates who can explain not only how they built ranking or retrieval systems, but how they measured and improved them. Penalize or de-prioritize profiles that claim senior ranking/search ownership with no evaluation language.
