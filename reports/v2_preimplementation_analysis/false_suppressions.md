## Patterns

- V1 under-separates candidates with evaluation/relevance-science evidence because evaluation is not a first-class score family.
- Strong ranking or retrieval ownership can be hidden under broad AI/ML/NLP titles.
- Some candidates with strong evidence are held back by consistency penalties or by market ordering after top-tail score saturation.
- V2 should promote source-diverse role-level ownership and evaluation metrics over generic title or skill breadth.
